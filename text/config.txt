<b>Region</b>: you can omit the 'region' argument when required.
<b>Summoner</b>: you can receive information from your summoner easier. This one needs the Region before.
<b>Notify</b>: you receive message from bot.
<b>Spectate</b>: you can receive information about the match you are playing. This one needs the Region and Summoner before.
