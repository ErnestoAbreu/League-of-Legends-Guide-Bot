Commands list:
/help: Shows everything the bot can do.
/config: Shows your settings.
/my_info: Shows info about your summoner.
/my_lastmatches: Shows info about last matches you played.
/my_current_match: Shows info about your current match.
/summoner: Shows info about a summoner.
/current_match: Shows info about the current match of a summoner.
/freechampions: Shows the current free champions.
/freechampions_fornewplayers: Shows the current free champions for new players.
/champion: Shows info about a champion.
/item: Shows info about a item.
/rune: Shows info about a rune.
/summoner_spell: Shows info about a summoner_spell.
